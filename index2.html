About Me

I am a researcher working in the field of Artificial Intelligence, focusing on designing methods for image and video restoration. My work involves applying advanced deep learning techniques such as diffusion models and GANs to problems like denoising, deblurring, super-resolution, and artifact removal.

I am currently working on video restoration and other multimedia projects, and previously focused on medical imaging (CT/MRI), developing models for low-dose CT denoising and artifact correction.

Outside of research, I enjoy travelling, playing cricket and football, and taking pictures.
